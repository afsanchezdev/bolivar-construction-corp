About Us

A family-owned builder, trusted since 2018

Bolivar Construction Corp is a family-run company that builds and remodels homes, as well as commercial stores, warehouses, and restaurants throughout South Florida. We've had the pleasure of working across cities including Miami Beach, Hialeah, and Miami.

We work with a broad portfolio of investors who trust us to develop their projects — duplexes, single-family homes, high-quality mansions, and pool & patio builds. Your ideas and dreams are important, so leave them in the best hands.
